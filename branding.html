MISSION
Design a simple and structured way for designers to learn user experience by focusing on and instead of tools and trends.
ABOUT

UXISM is a UX learning community started during an internship at IIT Delhi. It focuses on helping designers understand user experience in a simple and practical way.

PROCESS

The approach combines structured thinking with real-world practice. Instead of following trends, it focuses on fundamentals, user understanding, and clear problem solving.

The name: UXISM

UXISM comes from “UX” and “ism” where “ism” represents a movement, a shared belief, a way of thinking.
